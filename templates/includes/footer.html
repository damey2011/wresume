About

Wresume was created initially to manage my own resume and portfolio, and also avoid duplicating the same efforts creating for friends. Alas, I decided to make it open to every one who loves the product. It is free for now. Premium features would be added soon as it's still a work in progress, albeit safe for any kind of usage. Your data is safe and secured.
Also, there are future plans to open-source the application.
